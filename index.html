About Us

Liberty Property Management And Advisory Services Limited was incorporated under the company’s code of Ghana on the 23rd August 2013 as a private Company focused on Managing Real Estate Investments

For the visionary who dreams of having sufficient regular income without having to work actively for their basic needs, purchasing a Liberty managed property in a country with a 1.7 million housing deficit is guaranteed to yield great returns through managed rentals and resale.

Our Vision is to become the Market Leader in Property Management to meet the investor’s needs, expectations and shared vision of excellence.

To achieve this vision, the company provides expert real estate Investment advisory and property management services
